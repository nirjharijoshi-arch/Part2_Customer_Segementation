# Retention Strategy Report

## Segment 1: Champions

### Customer Behaviour

Recent purchasers with high purchase frequency and high monetary value.

### Recommended Action

VIP rewards, referral incentives, early-access promotions.

### Expected Business Value

High lifetime value retention and advocacy.

### Budget Priority

Medium.

---

## Segment 2: Loyal Customers

### Customer Behaviour

Frequent customers with strong engagement.

### Recommended Action

Personalized recommendations and loyalty benefits.

### Expected Business Value

Revenue stability and repeat purchases.

### Budget Priority

Medium.

---

## Segment 3: Potential Loyalists

### Customer Behaviour

Recently active customers with moderate spending.

### Recommended Action

Cross-sell campaigns and category-specific offers.

### Expected Business Value

Conversion into Loyal Customers.

### Budget Priority

High.

---

## Segment 4: At Risk

### Customer Behaviour

Historically active customers showing declining engagement.

### Recommended Action

Win-back campaigns and targeted discounts.

### Expected Business Value

Recovery of customers before churn.

### Budget Priority

Very High.

---

## Segment 5: Hibernating

### Customer Behaviour

Long periods of inactivity and extremely high churn risk.

### Recommended Action

Aggressive reactivation campaigns, special incentives, direct outreach.

### Expected Business Value

Reduction in customer attrition.

### Budget Priority

Highest.

---

## Budget Prioritization

1. Hibernating
2. At Risk
3. Potential Loyalists
4. Loyal Customers
5. Champions
