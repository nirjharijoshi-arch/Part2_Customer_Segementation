# Manual Review Cases

## Purpose

These customers were flagged for manual review because their behavioral signals present conflicting indications of churn risk. Automated segmentation alone may not provide sufficient context for decision-making.

---

## Case 1 – CUST00152

**Segment:** Loyal Customers

**Observation:**

* Frequency: 3 purchases
* Monetary Value: 2730.26
* Support Tickets: 3
* Campaign Clicks: 3

**Reason for Review:**
The customer demonstrates strong engagement with marketing campaigns while simultaneously generating a high number of support tickets. Further investigation is needed to determine whether support issues could negatively impact retention.
